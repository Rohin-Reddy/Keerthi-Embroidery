About us

A small business with a love for detail

Kreethi Embroidery celebrates Indian hand-finished aesthetics through computer embroidery designs and tassel works. We prepare practical products for boutiques, tailors, and individual customers who want festive, elegant, and easy-to-order craft items.

Our approach is simple: clear cataloging, fast messaging, and beautiful presentation. This demo site shows how a small craft business can sell online without relying on heavy platforms or backend complexity.

What we value

  • Thoughtful Indian-inspired color palettes
  • Fast mobile ordering and WhatsApp support
  • Transparent pricing in ₹ format
  • Easy manual updates to products and images
